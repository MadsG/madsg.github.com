Welcome to What's That Smell!

We've created a personalized smell test that comes with 10 different scents in every kit. The smell code is located under the cap and easily tells our database which 10 smells you have purchased so it can give you the proper data for those smells. This test is to help you see how good your sense of smell is compared to others. You are able to see information about your strength of sense in the category you were assigned. This test may also help medically because studies have shown that people who lose their sense of smell could be suseptable to early onset alzheimer's or parkinson's disease. We don't want to freak you out or anything but it is a good thing to know if you don't already. Mainly just have fun and keep smelling!
